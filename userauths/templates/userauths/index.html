About COOP

COOP (Co-operative Education) merupakan program yang menggabungkan pendidikan berbasis kelas dengan pengalaman kerja praktis. Mahasiswa yang mengikuti program ini akan melakukan kerja di perusahaan dalam durasi minimal 1 semester. Melalui COOP, diharapkan nantikan mahasiswa akan lebih siap dalam menghadapi dunia kerja setelah lulus karena telah memiliki pengalaman dan keahlian yang dibutuhkan pada saat kerja nanti

Universitas dapat menunjuk supervisor magang, serta industri juga dapat menunjuk supervisor untuk setiap siswa di tempat magang mereka. Melalui Prasmul Co-OP, akan lebih mudah bagi institusi dan industri untuk memantau dan mengevaluasi magang siswa. Kinerja siswa dapat dilaporkan secara berkala melalui laporan kinerja. Melalui laporan kinerja ini, supervisor magang dapat memantau kemajuan belajar siswa selama magang.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use this web?

Student Internship, Lecturer and Industry. student Internship mostly consists of Universitas Prasetiya Mulya Student who has Internship as Subject a.k.a 7th Semester Praseitiya Mulya Student. Lecturer consists of all of lecturer available in Prasetiya Mulya, whose ready to guide student through internship process. Industry consists of any company that has cooperation with Prasetiya Mulya or has Prasetiya Mulya Student working with them. Each Industry will need to assign HRD and Supervisor to use this services.

How to register to Prasmul Co-OP? (as a Company)

Companies HRD can register via Prasetiya Mulya CDC to get Co-OP Account.

How to register an internship? (as a Student)

Student Internship can register to internship via Prasmul Eli or by clicking on Register Internship on Prasmul Co-OP Student Dashboard.

How to manage internship administration?

Student Internship can manage Internship Administration after Internship Registration completed through Student Dashboard. Internship Administration including Attendance, Logbook, etc. Beside Student Internship, Lecturer, HRD and Supervisor are able to manage Internship Administration as well through their respective Dashboard.
